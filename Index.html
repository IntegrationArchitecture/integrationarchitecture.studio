Philosophy

Integration Architecture is the practice of designing systems where materials, light, behavior, and technology operate as one. The work is multi‑domain, experiential, and architectural in nature.

Experiential Systems

Objects, environments, and interactions designed as unified experiences rather than isolated components.

Material Intelligence

Working with materials as active participants in the experience, not passive surfaces.

Behavioral Design

Systems that respond, adapt, and evolve based on presence, context, and intention.

Architectural Integration

Design that respects space, proportion, and emotional logic—quiet, intentional, and precise.
